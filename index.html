Introducing The Venture

Welcome to your favorite part of the trip.

What is it?

The Venture is the first ultralight backpacking quilt produced by Ben Coleman and Will James.

It is constructed of high-quality, lightweight materials. Argon 90 is a lightweight, breathable material used for the outshell and Argon 67 is a thinner, softer type material to swaddle you in warm bliss.

Where can you use it?

The Venture is designed to go wherever you seek. With a warm down fill, you can take this quilt down to 15 degrees and still be cozy!
